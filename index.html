DealsFusion


With the eCommerce going main stream, there are enormous number of website that offer deals on weekly, daily and even hourly basis. Everyone wants to be the first to know and seal a deal! However, a laundry list of hundreds of sites to watch for on regular intervals makes it monotonous and a painful activity considering time being one the limiting factors.

Wouldn't it be great if you could free your email from a subscription spam on one hand and still could look at all the relevant deals while saving time doing it? If your answer is yes, then this innovolution would just be conducive for you! This application will manage all the deals accumulation for you and roll it up to one stop shop. It is also intended to learn (user would be able to opt out) your deal surfing preferences and over the period provide you with most relevant deals to optimize & save your time in the process.

One never knows when s/he could sneak in some time for checking out the deals and when that happens the resources to do so might not be accessible! This application would be available on all mediums be it web, or applications on mobiles, tablets and desktop so that you are never left wanting during free time. The roadmap also includes opening up a service/API based access to this solution for further discoveries that would want to benefit from this!
